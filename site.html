From the electronic rock of Radiohead, to Middle Earth epics of Led Zeppelin. From the depressed pop of The Smiths, to mad experimentations of Jimi Hendrix. One thread connects these great acts of rock and roll- the letter "J". This site is devoted to introducing new listeners to the work of four great guitarists whose name start with "J".
