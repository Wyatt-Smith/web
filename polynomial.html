My favorite polynomial

My favorite polynomial is 3x^4 + x^3 + 4x^2 + x + 5 because the coefficients are the first five digits of pi. The first five digits of pi are the ones I remember, and they neatly fit into the constraint of three to five terms. Pi is a transcendental irrational number, meaning that it goes on forever without repeating and can't be expressed in terms of roots. I still can't really wrap my brain around that.
