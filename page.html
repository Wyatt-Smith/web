Jimmy Page was guitarist for the legendery rock band, Led Zeppelin. Armed with his Les Paul and overdriven tube amps, Page has written timeless riffs and melodic solos. He also engineered the groups recordings, innovating with reverb and panning to get the band sounding as big as possible. His style is marked by the pentatonic scale and power chords.
